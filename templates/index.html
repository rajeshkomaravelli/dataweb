Genetic testing and analysis

Genetic testing is a type of medical test that analyses an individual’s DNA to identify changes or variations in genes, chromosomes or proteins. It provides information regarding inherited traits, risk factors and potential response to medicines. The results of a genetic test can confirm or rule out a suspected genetic condition or help determine a person’s chance of developing or passing on a genetic disorder. Genetic tests are generally performed on a sample of blood, hair, skin, amniotic fluid or other tissue. This test includes Studying of dna sequences to find mutations that aggravate the risk of the disorder, Analysing chromosomes to find any mutations that cause the disorder, Studying biochemistry of proteins to find any irrationalities that trigger the condition.

Bioinformatics tools and software

Bioinformatics tools are useful aids for comparing, analysing and interpreting genetic and genomic data. They are specifically designed for the extraction of meaningful information. They are also crucial in the understanding the evolutionary aspects of molecular biology. Bioinformatics enables us to handle the huge amounts of data involved and helps in finding common patterns . Bioinformatics involves processing, storing and analysing biological data.

Drug discovery and development

Drug discovery is a process that aims at identifying a small synthetic molecule or a large biomolecule and developing new medications to treat diseases. The overall process from discovery to marketing of a drug can take 10 to 15 years. Drug databases are collections of information regarding drugs,its targets,activities etc. They provide access to a wealth of information that aids in target identification, compound screening, structure-based drug design, and exploring relationships between drugs, targets, and biological activities.

Agricultural biotechnology

Agricultural biotechnology (agritech) is a field of agricultural science which includes a wide range of scientific tools and techniques, including traditional breeding techniques, that alter living organisms, or parts of organisms, to make or modify products. Crop biotechnology is a highly developed field of agricultural biotechnology.

Stem cell research

Stem-cell research is the area of research that studies the properties of stem cells and their potential use in medicine. As stem cells are the source of all tissues, understanding their properties helps in our understanding of the healthy and diseased body's development and homeostasis. The future of stem cell research is undeniably promising, with advancements in bone marrow transplants, cardiac tissue engineering, liver cell regeneration, and disease treatment on the horizon. Biomedical diagnostics is the study of the procedures that provide information to aid the screening, detection, diagnosis and monitoring of disease.
